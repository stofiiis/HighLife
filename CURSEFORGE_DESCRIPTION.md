# HighLife
by **stofiiis**

## English
HighLife is a NeoForge content mod for Minecraft 1.21.11 focused on a full cannabis progression loop: collect seeds, grow plants, harvest buds, dry them, breed better seeds, and use different smoking tools.

### Core Gameplay
- Wild seed drops from grass and fern blocks.
- Cannabis crop with multiple growth stages.
- Harvest loop with buds and seeds.
- Drying Rack block with timed processing.
- Seed Mixer block with breeding system and progress-based crafting.
- Optional bonemeal boost for faster seed mixing.

### Smoking System
- Joint, Pipe, Bong, and Weed Brownie items.
- Tool-specific behavior:
- Joint uses multiple puffs.
- Pipe uses bowl loading + short reload delay.
- Bong uses bowl loading + water refill mechanics.
- Custom sounds and custom first-person/third-person usage animations.

### Genetics and Progression
- Strain and quality data for seeds and buds.
- Seed classes from Common to Premium.
- Stackable seeds when strain/class data match.
- Peace effect window and related combat limitations.
- Built-in advancement progression for farming, smoking, loading, and breeding milestones.

### Technical Info
- Mod Loader: NeoForge
- Minecraft Version: 1.21.11
- Mod Version: 1.0.0
- Mod ID: `highlife`

## Cesky
HighLife je NeoForge content mod pro Minecraft 1.21.11 zamereny na kompletni loop kolem konopi: ziskani seedu, pestovani, sklizen, suseni, slechtni lepsich seedu a pouzivani ruznych nastroju na huleni.

### Hlavni Gameplay
- Drop seedu z travy a kapradi.
- Konopna kytka s vice rustovymi fazemi.
- Sklizen budu a seedu.
- Drying Rack block s casovanym zpracovanim.
- Seed Mixer block pro slechtni s progressem.
- Volitelny bonemeal boost pro rychlejsi michani seedu.

### Smoking System
- Joint, Pipe, Bong a Weed Brownie.
- Kazdy nastroj se chova jinak:
- Joint ma vice potahu.
- Pipe funguje pres nabijeni bowl + kratky delay.
- Bong funguje pres nabijeni bowl + doplnovani vody.
- Vlastni zvuky a vlastni animace pouziti (first person i third person).

### Genetika a Progres
- Strain a quality data na seedu i budech.
- Kategorie seedu od Common po Premium.
- Stackovani seedu pri shodnem strainu/tride.
- Peace efekt s docasnym omezenim boje.
- Advancement progres pro pestovani, huleni, nabijeni i slechtni.

- Verze Minecraftu: 1.21.11
