## English
HighLife is a NeoForge content mod for Minecraft 1.21.11 focused on a full mystic herb progression loop: collect seeds, grow plants, harvest bundles, dry them, breed better seeds, and use custom infusion tools.

> **Core Loop:** Seed drop -> Grow -> Harvest -> Dry -> Infuse
> **Breeding:** Seed Mixer with optional bonemeal speed boost
> **Progression:** Strain, quality, seed classes, advancements

### Core Gameplay
- Wild seed drops from grass and fern blocks.
- Mystic herb crop with multiple growth stages.
- Harvest loop with bundles and seeds.
- Drying Rack block with timed processing.
- Seed Mixer block with breeding system and progress-based crafting.
- Optional bonemeal boost for faster seed mixing.

### Tool System
- Herb Roll, Infusion Wand, Alchemy Flask, and Herb Cookie items.
- Tool-specific behavior:
- Herb Roll uses multiple charges.
- Infusion Wand uses bowl loading + short reload delay.
- Alchemy Flask uses bowl loading + water refill mechanics.
- Custom sounds and custom first-person/third-person usage animations.

### Genetics and Progression
- Strain and quality data for seeds and bundles.
- Seed classes from Common to Premium.
- Stackable seeds when strain/class data match.
- Peace effect window and related combat limitations.
- Built-in advancement progression for farming, loading, hydration, and breeding milestones.

### Technical Info

- Minecraft Version: 1.21.11
- Mod Version: 1.0.0

## Cesky
HighLife je NeoForge content mod pro Minecraft 1.21.11 zamereny na kompletni loop kolem mystickych bylin: ziskani seedu, pestovani, sklizen, suseni, slechteni lepsich seedu a pouzivani vlastnich infuznich nastroju.

> **Core Loop:** Drop seedu -> Pestovani -> Sklizen -> Suseni -> Infuze
> **Slechteni:** Seed Mixer s volitelnym bonemeal zrychlenim
> **Progres:** Strain, quality, seed tridy, advancementy

### Hlavni Gameplay
- Drop seedu z travy a kapradi.
- Mysticka bylina s vice rustovymi fazemi.
- Sklizen bundlu a seedu.
- Drying Rack block s casovanym zpracovanim.
- Seed Mixer block pro slechteni s progresem.
- Volitelny bonemeal boost pro rychlejsi michani seedu.

### Nastroje
- Herb Roll, Infusion Wand, Alchemy Flask a Herb Cookie.
- Kazdy nastroj se chova jinak:
- Herb Roll ma vice nabiti.
- Infusion Wand funguje pres nabijeni bowl + kratky delay.
- Alchemy Flask funguje pres nabijeni bowl + doplnovani vody.
- Vlastni zvuky a vlastni animace pouziti (first person i third person).

### Genetika a Progres
- Strain a quality data u seedu i bundlu.
- Kategorie seedu od Common po Premium.
- Stackovani seedu pri shodnem strainu/tride.
- Peace efekt s docasnym omezenim boje.
- Advancement progres pro pestovani, nabijeni, hydrataci i slechteni.

### Technicke Info
- Mod Loader: NeoForge
- Verze Minecraftu: 1.21.11
- Verze modu: 1.0.0
- Mod ID: `highlife`
